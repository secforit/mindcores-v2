# SELF-ESTEEM

In today’s world, people are overloaded with messages about how they should look and behave, which is why self-esteem is something that many of people struggle with. But maintaining a positive self image is critical for the own mental health and overall well-being.

## What Is The Meaning Of Self-Esteem?

Self-esteem refers to the overall sense of self-worth and the ability to accept and value oneself. It’s the opinion or judgment of oneself and the own abilities, and it can have a significant impact on behavior, emotions, and relationships. When having high self-esteem, people tend to have positive attitudes and beliefs about themselves, while low self-esteem brings about feelings of insecurity and inadequacy. It is important to note that self-esteem can fluctuate and can be influenced by external factors such as social interactions and life experiences.

## What Are The 3 Types Of Self-Esteem?

**High self-esteem** is characterised by a positive self-image and confidence in the own abilities. High self-esteem means a healthy balance between self-acceptance, self-love and self-improvement.

**Moderate self-esteem** can mean an inconsistent self-image and self-worth. Having moderate self-esteem people may have moments of confidence and self-doubt.

**Low self-esteem** leads to a negative self-image and self-worth. Have low self-esteem people tend to have a consistently negative self-perception and may have difficulty accepting compliments or positive feedback.

## What Things Improve Self-Esteem?

Improving self-esteem can be beneficial for all of us, therefore are some ways that Self-Esteem can be improved.

### Practice Self-Care

Taking care of one’s physical and emotional well-being can help improve self-esteem.

## Set Goals And Work Towards Achieving Them

Setting and achieving goals can help individuals feel a sense of accomplishment and boost self-esteem.

### Challenge Negative Thoughts

Recognizing and challenging negative thoughts and beliefs can help individuals develop a more positive self-image.

### Surround Yourself With Supportive People

Surrounding oneself with supportive and positive individuals can help individuals develop a more positive self-image.

### Practice Self-Compassion

Being kind and understanding towards oneself can help individuals develop a more positive self-image and improve self-esteem.
