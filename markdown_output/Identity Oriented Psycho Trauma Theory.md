# Identity Oriented Psycho Trauma Theory (IoPT)

Healing from either physical or mental disorders needs a goal. According to Prof. Dr. Ruppert this "equates to fully and completely living one's own identity, so that we can express and realise what is at the core of our being". In order to be in touch with our own identity we "need a healthy self, a free will and to be in close emotional contact with our body".

The IoPT framework provides a safe way to explore the suppressed and unconscious parts of the psyche, to recover from the effects of trauma and to restore our healthy self. The central focus in this therapy is on a personal life and an in-depth discovery of one's own identity.

The framework shows how trauma affects the whole LHO ( living human organism) and how to recover from the effect of trauma to gain a healthy self and a healthy will, despite traumatisation."

"IoPT therapy is based on a specific understanding of trauma, and psychological splitting as the means of surviving trauma"
