„Checkliste Trauma“ from Prof. Dr. Franz Ruppert in Englisch.

Englisch:

* Intergenerational Trauma:

- My mother's/father's mother is traumatized;
- My mother's/father's father is traumatized;
- My mother/father is traumatized.

* Trauma of Identity:

- I was unwanted as a child by my mother/father;
- I survived an attempted abortion;
- I experienced violence in my mother's womb;
- I suffer from panic attacks/chronic anxiety;
- I have the "wrong" gender for my parents;
- My will was broken through violence;
- I seek my identity externally;
- I want to be better than others;
- I was given up for adoption.

Englisch:

* Trauma of Love:

- I didn't receive the love I needed;
- I have to be there for/save my parents;
- I still hope to be loved; I feel guilty;
- I am obsessed with what others think;
- I'm looking for my place in the family;
- Fits of rage.

* Trauma of Sexuality:

- Genital mutilation;
- Sexual assault/violence;
- Violence during childbirth;
- No childhood memories;
- Chronic illnesses;
- Rejection of the body;
- Feeling nothing;
- Drug use;
- Depressive or aggressive.

* Trauma of Own Perpetration:

- Aborted a child;
- Committed violence against others;
- Didn't give my children love;
- Sexually traumatized my child;
- Life-threatening conflicts;
- Feelings of guilt and shame.

* Trauma of Loss:

- Lost a parent/sibling/child early.

* Guide for a Healthy Identity:

- Stable contact with my self;
- Having my own will;
- Feeling what belongs to me;
- Love without self-abandonment;
- Can accept love;
- Critical thinking skills;
- Liking my body/sexuality;
- No fear of memories;
- Distinguishing reality from projections;
- Constructive relationships.
